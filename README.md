# 📊 RetailPulse BI Dashboard
### Smart Sales & Performance Analytics for Multi-Category Retail Businesses

> **RetailPulse BI Dashboard** is a comprehensive Power BI solution designed to transform raw retail sales data into actionable business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, evaluate product profitability, compare time periods, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions across multiple product categories.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

### Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, without a centralized analytical solution, decision-makers often struggle to:

- Identify top and underperforming products
- Track sales and profitability trends
- Measure the impact of discounts and promotions
- Compare business performance across different periods
- Understand geographical sales distribution
- Analyze order-level details efficiently

### Solution

The **RetailPulse BI Dashboard** was developed using **Power BI** to provide an interactive and user-friendly analytical platform that consolidates sales, profit, customer, product, and geographic performance metrics into a single solution.

### Business Value

The dashboard helps:

- Executives monitor business performance
- Sales managers identify revenue opportunities
- Product teams optimize inventory decisions
- Marketing teams evaluate promotional effectiveness
- Operations teams analyze customer purchasing behavior

# 🧮 DAX Measures

Common DAX calculations used:

```DAX
Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Total Sales])

Total Profit = SUM(Sales[Profit])

Net Sales = SUM(Sales[Net Sales])

Quantity Sold = SUM(Sales[Units Sold])

Orders Count = DISTINCTCOUNT(Sales[Order ID])

Average Discount =
AVERAGE(Sales[Discount Percentage])

Profit Margin % =
DIVIDE([Total Profit],[Net Sales],0)
```
